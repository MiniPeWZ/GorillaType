Machida Miki Comissioned Build

A custom build with the GMK KITSUNE set

The build is based on the mythical nine tailed foxes which are believed to be extremely wise and having the ability to shape shift. The huge novelty set tries to bring out some faces of the mystical creatures.

We have included two sets of accents : Sand accents by default represent the arena while the blood red accents (only Novs) represent the actual battles fought there.

The major attraction of this build would be the color references to antique heavily patinaed armor, sand and blood. With the novelties set, bring back the roars of the ancient colosseum to your board and get mesmerized in the battle of the gods.
